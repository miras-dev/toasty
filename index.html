Friends, One Dream

We are four friends
from the Malabar coast.

Our friendship grew around food, travel, and long conversations that never really ended. Food was always the common ground — every plan, every journey, every dream somehow found its way back to a table.

Years later, two of us found ourselves in Berlin. A city built by stories, cultures, and people who came from somewhere else. Naturally, a question followed: "Why not start something of our own?"
