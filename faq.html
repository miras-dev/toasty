Visiting us

  • What are your opening hours?

    We are open Monday to Friday from 08:00 to 22:00, and on weekends from 09:00 to 23:00. We are closed on public holidays. Last food orders are taken 30 minutes before closing.

  • Where exactly are you located in Berlin?

    We are based in Berlin, Germany. The full address is available on our contact page and in Google Maps. We are well connected by public transport — U-Bahn and S-Bahn stops are a short walk away.

  • Is there outdoor seating?

    Yes — we have outdoor seating available in warmer months. It fills up quickly on sunny days, so we recommend arriving early or making a reservation if you'd prefer to be outside.

  • Is Toasty Turtle wheelchair accessible?

    Yes, our main dining area is fully accessible. If you have specific accessibility requirements, please reach out to us at hello@toastyturtle.de before your visit and we will do our best to accommodate you.

  • Can I bring my dog?

    Well-behaved dogs are welcome in our outdoor seating area. We keep a bowl of water by the door. Please keep your dog on a lead at all times and let us know when you book if you're bringing a furry guest.

  • Do you have Wi-Fi?

    Yes, free Wi-Fi is available for all guests. Ask any team member for the current password when you arrive. We love that people work from here — just order a coffee and make yourself at home.

  • Is there parking nearby?

    Street parking is available in the surrounding area. We are also very well connected by public transport, which we'd always recommend in Berlin. Cycling infrastructure nearby is excellent too.

  • Can I bring children?

    Absolutely. Toasty Turtle is a family-friendly space. We have high chairs available and are happy to adapt portions for younger guests. Just let us know when you arrive.

Food & reservations

  • Do I need a reservation?

    Walk-ins are always welcome. However, we do get busy on weekend mornings and Friday evenings. If you want to guarantee a specific table or time, we recommend booking via our reservation page or emailing hello@toastyturtle.de.

  • Is the food vegetarian or vegan-friendly?

    Most of our menu is vegetarian. Several dishes are fully vegan or can be made vegan on request — including our Kerala curry and shakshuka bowl. All allergens and dietary information are listed on the menu. Just ask your server if you're unsure.

  • Do you cater for allergies?

    Yes. We take allergies seriously. Please let us know about any allergies or intolerances when ordering, and our team will guide you through safe options. While we take every precaution, our kitchen does handle nuts, gluten, and dairy.

  • Can I book for a large group or private event?

    Yes — we love hosting groups. For parties of 8 or more, or for private event enquiries, please email us at hello@toastyturtle.de with your date, group size, and any special requirements, and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

  • Where does your food come from?

    We source produce from local Berlin markets and trusted suppliers wherever possible. Our spices are imported directly from Kerala — the same families and cooperatives our founders grew up with. Freshness and provenance matter deeply to us.

  • Do you serve alcohol?

    We keep a small, thoughtfully chosen selection of natural wines and craft beers. Our focus is on exceptional non-alcoholic drinks — the spiced chai, the cold brew latte, and our seasonal specials are worth the visit on their own.

  • Can I cancel or change a reservation?

    Of course. We ask that you let us know at least 4 hours in advance if you need to cancel or change your booking, so we can give your table to another guest. Email hello@toastyturtle.de or call us directly.

  • How do I use cookies on this site?

    We use cookies solely to improve your browsing experience on our website. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can accept or decline cookies via the banner that appears on your first visit. Your choice is saved locally in your browser.
